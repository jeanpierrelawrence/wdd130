South Africa

South Africa, at the southern tip of Africa, is a diverse, multi-ethnic nation known for its stunning biodiversity, rich mineral resources, and complex history, including the legacy of apartheid, transitioning to a constitutional democracy with three capitals (Pretoria, Cape Town, Bloemfontein) and 12 official languages, facing ongoing challenges like inequality and high crime alongside a developed economy. Nicknamed "A world in one country," it boasts varied landscapes from deserts to coastlines, supports unique wildlife, and is a major tourism destination, but grapples with economic disparities and social issues stemming from its past.
